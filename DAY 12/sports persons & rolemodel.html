My Role Models

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a fearless and wise king. He founded the Maratha Empire with strong leadership. He respected women and all religions equally. Chhatrapati Shivaji is a symbol of courage and justice.

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj was the brave son of Shivaji Maharaj. He faced enemies with unmatched bravery and pride. He refused to convert even under torture by the Mughals. Chhatrapati Sambhaji's sacrifice is remembered forever.

Rajmata Jijabai

Rajmata Jijabai was the mother of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. She raised him with values of dharma and bravery. Her strength and wisdom shaped a future king. Rajmata Jijabai is a true example of motherhood.

Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is a humble and visionary Indian businessman. He led the Tata Group to great global success. He believes in honesty, kindness, and social service. His life inspires many future leaders of India.

Dhirubhai Ambani

Dhirubhai Ambani started small and built a huge business. He showed India how to dream big and take risks. He changed the world of Indian industry forever. His journey motivates every young entrepreneur.
