My Top 5 Favourite Movies

The Godfather

This classic film tells the story of the Corleone family. It shows power, loyalty, and crime in the Mafia world. The acting and music are unforgettable and iconic. A must-watch for anyone who loves great storytelling.

The Wolf of Wall Street

This movie is based on the true story of Jordan Belfort. It shows the rise and fall of a stockbroker in New York. The film is filled with wild energy and bold acting. It’s both funny and a warning about greed.

The Pursuit of Happyness

This emotional film shows a father's struggle for success. Will Smith’s performance is inspiring and powerful. It teaches us to never give up on our dreams. Based on a true story of courage and hope.

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump is a simple man with a big heart. His journey through life is both funny and touching. He meets presidents and changes history unknowingly. It’s a beautiful story about kindness and destiny.

The Shawshank Redemption

This movie is about hope, friendship, and freedom. Andy Dufresne is wrongly jailed but never loses hope. He forms a deep bond with another prisoner, Red. The ending is powerful and unforgettable.
