My Role Model: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

His Early Days

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19 February 1630 at Shivneri Fort.
His mother, Jijabai, raised him with tales of courage and honor.
In a time of war and chaos, he saw his people’s struggles.
Young Shivaji dreamed of a land free from outside rule.


Dream of Freedom

Shivaji Maharaj had a vision of Swarajya, self-rule for all.
He took forts like Rajgad and built the Maratha Empire.
His bold goal inspired warriors to fight mighty kings.
He worked hard to free his people from oppression.

Clever Fighting

Shivaji Maharaj mastered Ganimi Kava, a smart battle trick.
He used mountains to hide and strike enemies fast.
Bigger armies, like Mughals, couldn’t stop his plans.
His clever fighting turned him into a true legend.


Big Wins

In 1670, he won at Sinhagad with Tanaji’s help.
They climbed steep walls to surprise the enemy force.
At Umberkhind, he trapped foes in a tight spot.
These victories made the Marathas strong and proud.

Ruling Fairly

Shivaji Maharaj ruled with his Ashta Pradhan council.
He made taxes fair so farmers could live well.
His rules brought peace and helped the poor.
People loved and trusted his kind leadership.


Protecting the Sea

He built a navy to guard the Konkan coast.
Forts like Sindhudurg kept enemies far away.
His ships saved trade from foreign attackers.
This kept his land safe and strong.

Becoming King
        What: Became King
        When: 6 June 1674
        Where: Raigad Fort
        Why: To lead Marathas

He became Chhatrapati in 1674 at Raigad.
This big event united all his people.
He ruled with strength to protect everyone.
His kindness made the kingdom thrive.


Caring for All

Shivaji Maharaj kept women safe from harm.
He gave fair respect to every religion.
His land was peaceful for all kinds.
He truly cared for each person.

His Lasting Mark

Shivaji Maharaj’s bravery still inspires us today.
He fought for freedom with all his might.
His story lives in tales and songs.
He shows me how to be tough.


Why I Like Him

I admire Shivaji Maharaj for his bravery always.
His good heart cared for the weak.
He was strong yet fair to all.
I want to follow his great ways.
